Documents for the frontier.

Workpads are documents that can be shared & viewed in browsers or apps - without passing through the Internet.

Document content is encoded & compressed into strings, creating shareable links. Each Workpad displays content locally using templates and can easily be secured using encrypted keys.

-----

Workpads is a branch of the 'Business Assistance System' (BASICS). Technical development of Workpads aligns with standards established for BASICS but will remain uneffected by upstream product designs & commercial partnerships.

To learn about our commitment to privacy see Computer Science of Sovereignty.
